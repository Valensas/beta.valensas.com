Bilyoner is Turkey's first electronic casino games platform, offering betting services, lottery ticket results, and banking services. We worked with Bilyoner on the following:

- Bilyoner’s integration with the central bidding system
- new content management system to support the Bilyoner’s mobile app
- live-betting feature
- development of a web socket
- development of a push notification feature
